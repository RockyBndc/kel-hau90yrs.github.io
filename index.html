ABOUT

Tracing the Past

Holy Angel University is a Catholic private institution located in Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. It was decided to name after the Holy Angels, the patron saints of Angeles City, and was established in 1933 by prominent philanthropist Don Juan Nepomuceno and and Fr. Pedro P. Santos, then parish priest of the town of Angeles and later Archbishop of Nueva Caceres.

The university was formerly established as Holy Angel Academy, a high school. In 1938, it later grew into an independent college with programs in liberal arts, business, and education. After acquiring full university classification in 1962, the university has kept growing and adding new programs ever since.

Holy Angel University has consistently stayed dedicated to offering high-quality instruction that incorporates faith and morals into the curriculum. Also, it has taken a lead role in fostering social justice, human rights, and environmental conservation in the neighborhood.

Being one of the top universities in the Philippines today, Holy Angel University provides a variety of courses for both undergraduate and graduate students in a number of academic fields. To improve its educational and research skills, it has also formed relationships with several other universities and organizations all over the globe.

The 90 Years of Angelites' Transcendence

LUID - a Kapampangan word which means “Long Live” .

Holy Angel University continues to be a leading institution in Central Luzon after 90 years of existence. The fact that the institution has been able to develop leaders who are capable of operating on a global scale over the years says volumes about the impact it continues to inspire among its learners.

This represents the Angelites' transcendent splendor over a period of more than 90 years. Witnessing and experiencing how each and every Angelite, as well as the university, overcomes the challenges of life and time, continuing to uphold its unwavering mission and vision.

As the HAU gates are reopened, we may all relive this spectacular and joyous occasion while creating our fondest memories here for another 90 years.
